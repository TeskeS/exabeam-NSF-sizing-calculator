Average Log Size

To get the average byte size per log, follow these steps:

Remote access into the XM/platform manager.

Open a browser and pull up:

https://localhost:3000

Select Mediator. If your Grafana doesn’t look like my screenshot, you can search “mediator” in the search bar to locate the dashboard.

The Mediator: Processing widget will show the Average Log Size in Bytes for the timeframe of your choosing. I recommend a metric timeframe of at least 2 weeks if not greater.

If your dashboard doesn’t have a “Processing” widget, there should be another widget that shows the “Avg Log Size” in KiB.
